FRUITY TREASURES

Delivering hard-to-find, affordable fruit boxes to your doorstep.




We wanted to create a website for our ideated fruit subscription box, Fruity Treasures, that emphasized the company's passion for cultural diversity and accessibility in the US produce industry. My role in design revolved around not only prioritizing these values, but also creating a site that was reflective of and relatable to the target user.
